Falaknuma Coders
Dashboard Disease Prediction Upload your x-ray and check for presence of any disease
Falaknuma Coders is an app that allows the user to upload his/her x-ray image to check for the presence of any potential disease leveraging custom trained deep learing neural network in the background. The x-ray scanning application allows the user/doctor to check for the presence of any of the following diseases:
Atelectasis | Cardiomegaly | Effusion | Infiltration | Mass | Nodule | Pneumonia
Pneumothorax | Consolidation | Edema | Emphysema | Fibrosis | Pleural Thickening | Hernia
